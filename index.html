Short Bio

Shinichirō Watanabe was born on May 24, 1965 in Kyoto, Japan. He is a Japanese anime filmmaker, screenwriter, and producer. Director of multiple critically acclaimed anime series including Cowboy Bepop, Samurai Champloo, Carole and Tuesday, and Space Dandy. Watanabe often utilizes multiple genres into an anime series. Watanabe accentuates a specific musical genre in each of his major tv series. Watanabe believes music is a universal language, and is very particular and distinct about using the proper score/songs in his animes.
