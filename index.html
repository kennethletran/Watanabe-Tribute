animation Shinichiro Watanabe

Cowboy Bepop

1998 - 1999 Produced by Sunrise TV-14 | Action, Adventure 26 Episodes

One of the first to introduce mature themes which captivated many fans especially in the West. With Yoko Kanno's stellar jazz soundtrack alongside a stacked cast, this anime belted out memorable dialogue, Bruce Lee inspired combat sequences, and epic gunfights.

Samurai Champloo

2004 - 2005 Produced by Manglobe TV-MA | Action, Adventure 26 Episodes

An iconic amalgamation of hip-hop and samurais. One of the few animes that I will shamelessly watch dub. Watch for the slick blade sequences, stay for the hilariously strange episodic plots and character dynamics. Rest in peace, Nujabes.

Space Dandy

2014 Produced by Bones TV-14 | Adventure, Comedy 26 Episodes

After seeing inklings of Watanabe's comedic flair in Cowboy Bepop and Samurai Champloo, he flexes his funny bone by creating a trippy, silly adventure anime centered around a moronic trio. It shouldn't be but, "Welcome to the X Dimension" is so damn catchy...I love it.
